# BLXST Content Proposal Factory Policy v0

Purpose: turn runtime intake router selected routes into content proposals.

## Core Law

The factory is proposal-only.

It must not write D1, R2, KV, Workers, website source, registry, Cloudflare, git, or deployment state.

## Provenance Law

User-submitted facts require review before publish or apply.

AI extraction is structure only, not factual authority.

Unknown route shapes safe-stop into review proposal.
